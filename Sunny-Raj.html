WHO AM I

I'm Sunny Raj — a 1st year CSE student at AMC Engineering College, Bengaluru, originally from Patna, Bihar. Before I even stepped into college, I had already freelanced as a scriptwriter earning ₹10,000, competed in powerlifting hitting a 160kg deadlift PR, and built a creative voice online.

After joining AMC, I went all in. I've participated in 8+ hackathons, won one at Presidency University, ran a 21km half marathon and a 5000m race through VTU Athletics, and did marketing for ed-tech consultancy Neet Star.

I'm a filmmaker, a builder, a storyteller, and someone who doesn't sit still. Whether it's a script, a sprint, or a side project — I go all the way.
